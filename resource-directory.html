AI-assisted, human-approved

How AI keeps this maintained

AI can reduce the manual burden of keeping a resource directory current, but publication decisions should stay in a human review queue.

01

Discover resources

Scan official websites, 211 listings, agencies, schools, hospitals, and nonprofits for candidate services.

02

Check duplicates

Compare names, addresses, phone numbers, source domains, and service areas before adding a listing.

03

Summarize services

Draft plain-language descriptions, hours, eligibility notes, and audience tags from authoritative sources.

04

Verify links

Check URLs, redirects, status codes, and whether source pages still support the listing.

05

Flag stale information

Prioritize records with aging verification dates, changed pages, or uncertain phone and hours details.

06

Human review before publishing

Route proposed additions and edits to staff for approval before residents see updates.

Future workflow preview

Submit or correct a resource

This demo does not collect submissions yet, but a production portal could route suggested additions, corrections, and provider updates into a review queue.

Example review queue steps

  1. Resident or provider submits an update.
  2. AI checks for duplicates and missing fields.
  3. Staff reviewer confirms source and eligibility details.
  4. Approved change publishes with a new verification date.
